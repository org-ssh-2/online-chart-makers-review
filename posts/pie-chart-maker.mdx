# Pie Chart Makers

Pie charts are a popular way to display data and make comparisons between different factors. These simple and easy-to-understand charts show how much each category or factor contributes to the entire data set. While pie charts can be created manually using a ruler, compass, and a protractor, it's much more convenient to use a pie chart maker.

A pie chart maker is a tool that allows users to create pie charts online or offline. It automatically calculates the percentages and creates a visually stunning chart that can be exported in various formats such as PNG, JPG, PDF or SVG. Online pie chart makers are browser-based and available for free. They come with a variety of options and customization features so you can create a pie chart that's perfect for your needs.

## 1. Google Charts

Google Charts is a popular tool that can be used to create pie charts and other types of charts, including bar charts, line charts, scatter plots, and more. This free service comes with a simple and easy-to-use interface that allows you to customize every aspect of your pie chart. You can style your chart, set the colors of each pie slice, labels, and more. Moreover, it can also be integrated easily with other Google services and APIs.

One of the best things about Google Charts is that you don't need any programming skills to use it. It has a drag-and-drop interface and you can easily upload a CSV file or use Google Sheets to import your data.

## 2. Canva

Canva is a popular graphic design platform that can be used to create professional-looking pie charts. It is available as a web application and also has a mobile app. The best thing about Canva is that it comes with tons of pre-made templates that can be customized to create your own customized pie charts. You can also upload your own data and create a pie chart from scratch using the drag-and-drop interface. Moreover, it offers a library of fonts, icons, and images to further customize your chart.

Canva offers a free version with a limited set of features. However, you can access more features and templates by subscribing to the premium plan.

## 3. Infogram

Infogram is an online chart creator that offers a range of charts including pie charts, bar charts, line charts, and more. It has a free version with limited features, but you can access more features by subscribing to the premium plan. Infogram offers a user-friendly interface with the ability to upload data from various sources including Excel, Google Sheets, Dropbox and many more.

The real-time data visualization feature of Infogram allows you to make updates instantly, which is a great feature for businesses that rely on real-time data. You can also easily embed the charts you create on your website or share them on social media.

## 4. Datawrapper

Datawrapper is another cloud-based [pie chart maker](https://docs.kanaries.net/charts/pie-chart-maker) that is widely used by individuals, businesses, and non-profits to create charts for presentations, reports, or infographics. It offers a user-friendly and clean interface that makes creating a pie chart simple and easy.

The company offers a free version with limited features, but you can access more features by subscribing to the premium plan. One of the main benefits of Datawrapper is that it offers a variety of customizable themes, templates, and styles, making it easy to create pie charts that suit your needs.

## 5. Plotly

The final pie chart maker on our list is Plotly. It is a web-based service that enables you to create interactive and customizable pie charts and a range of other visualizations. Plotly focuses on data-driven storytelling and allows you to easily upload your data, filter, analyze it and create a pie chart of your choice.

Plotly is open source and offers both a free and paid version with more features. It has a range of customizable templates and unique features that make creating pie charts more engaging and interactive. Moreover, it also offers an API that allows you to integrate your charts with third-party applications like R, Python, and Matlab.

## Conclusion

Overall, pie chart makers are a convenient and easy-to-use tool that allows you to create visually stunning charts that are perfect for presentations, reports, and infographics. Each of the pie chart makers discussed has their unique features and benefits that make them suitable for different purposes. It is up to you to decide which one works best for your needs.
