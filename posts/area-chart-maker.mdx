## What is an Area Chart?

An area chart is a type of chart that displays data as a series of points connected by a line. The area between the line and the x-axis is usually filled with color to emphasize the magnitude of the data. Area charts are commonly used for depicting trends over time, comparing datasets, and identifying patterns in the data.

## Advantages of Using an Area Chart

1. Easy to Interpret: Area charts are easy to read and interpret, even for non-experts. The color-fill area highlights the magnitude of the data, making the chart more intuitive.

2. Visualize Trends: Area charts are ideal for visualizing trends over time. By connecting points with a line, it becomes easier to see changes in the data.

3. Comparison: Area charts can also be used to compare two or more datasets. By superimposing these charts on top of each other, you can easily identify patterns and spot differences between them.
